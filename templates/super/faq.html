Q1: Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! Every Jomivic Travel package can be tailored to your destinations, accommodations, activities, and travel dates to match your preferences and budget.

Q2: How do I book a trip?

Booking is easy! Choose a package online or contact our travel experts. Once your itinerary is finalized, secure your trip using our safe online payment system.

Q3: Do you offer group bookings?

Yes! We provide custom packages for families, friends, or corporate groups. Contact us to plan your group trip.

Q4: Can I extend my trip or add extra destinations?

Many of our packages are flexible. Let us know your desired changes, and we'll adjust your itinerary accordingly.

Q5: Is my booking secure?

Absolutely. We use secure payment gateways and trusted partners to ensure your information and payment are fully protected.

Q6: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, online banking, and popular digital wallets. Custom arrangements are possible for group trips.

Q7: Do you offer refunds or cancellations?

Policies vary by package and supplier. Contact us promptly if your plans change, and we'll guide you through available options. View our full Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Q8: Do you help with visas and travel documents?

Yes! Our team can guide you through visa requirements and document preparation for most destinations.

Q9: Do I need travel insurance?

While optional, travel insurance is strongly recommended to protect against delays, emergencies, or unforeseen events.

Q10: Can I get help after booking?

Of course! Our support team is available before, during, and after your trip for questions, changes, or emergencies.

Q11: What if I have special requirements (diet, accessibility, etc.)?

We can accommodate most special needs. Let us know during booking so we can make the necessary arrangements.

Q12: Are flights included in your packages?

Some packages include flights, while others are land-only. Check each package description or contact us for details.

Q13: How are accommodations selected?

We select hotels and stays based on quality, location, and traveler feedback. Customization is possible to match your preferences.

Q14: Are local guides included?

Many packages include professional local guides who provide cultural insights and ensure a smooth experience.

Q15: What about meals and dietary preferences?

Meal plans vary by package. Options may include breakfast, half-board, or full-board. Vegetarian and other dietary preferences can usually be accommodated.
